# 초5 파닉스 왕초보 보강팩 (듣기/말하기 기초 포함)

> 대상: 파닉스를 어려워하는 초5
> 목표: 4주 동안 "소리-읽기" 불안 감소 + 짧은 문장 따라 말하기

## 1) 4주 플랜

### Week 1: 단모음 + CVC
- a/e/i/o/u 소리 구분
- cat, bed, pig, hot, sun

### Week 2: silent e
- cap→cape, kit→kite, hop→hope

### Week 3: 자주 쓰는 자음 결합
- ch, sh, th, ck, ng
- chip, ship, thin, back, sing

### Week 4: 이중모음 기초
- ai, ee, oa, ou, oi
- rain, tree, boat, out, coin

---

## 2) 하루 20분 루틴
1. 소리 7분
2. 단어 읽기 7분
3. 듣고 따라말하기 4분
4. 체크 2분

---

## 3) 듣고 말하기(왕초보) 문장 20개
1. I am ready.
2. This is easy.
3. I can read.
4. I like this book.
5. It is my pen.
6. I have a cat.
7. The sun is hot.
8. I see a ship.
9. This is a thin book.
10. I can sing.
11. I need help.
12. Please say it again.
13. Let’s read together.
14. I can do it.
15. I am okay.
16. Today is fun.
17. I like school.
18. This is my class.
19. I will try again.
20. Good job, me.

활용법:
- 하루 3~5문장만 듣고 따라 읽기
- 속도보다 정확한 소리 우선

---

## 4) 최소 단어 리스트 60 (4주)

cat, cap, bed, pen, pig, pin, hot, pot, sun, run, map, bag, sit, lip, cup

### Week2 (15)
cape, name, bike, kite, note, home, cute, cube, make, fine, hope, rose, game, time, nose

### Week3 (15)
chip, shop, thin, this, check, back, duck, ring, king, song, fish, shell, bath, thing, strong

### Week4 (15)
rain, mail, tree, feet, boat, coat, out, mouth, coin, point, train, seed, road, cloud, toy

## 5) 부모 코칭 문장 (짧게)
